"A busy working professional’s journey to a healthier lifestyle – the story of Anju Jayprakash"


When I took on fitness in 2016, I wasn’t sure how to begin. In general, I found myself feeling tired more often. I was looking for guidance on the best way to work out and lead a healthier lifestyle.
I attempted to set my own exercise schedules, but wasn’t able to follow them consistently. Before starting the program with Aadit Life, I wondered if this would be yet another attempt that would fizzle out later.
As someone who hasn’t had an organized workout plan in the past, I can say this was the best way for me to start and gain confidence. As structured as it was, the plan was also flexible enough to accommodate my ever-changing schedule; between work and having a school-going kid, I’m always caught up in late evenings, birthday parties, and projects.
Though the workouts demanded less of my time, I could see the results. I gained muscle mass, lost weight, and can run longer and faster than I ever have. There was also an overall spike in my energy levels.
Anand’s feedback system was my favourite part of the program. It monitors so many different impact measures apart fromgit chec just weight. This way, I’m able to adjust my workouts to run better and work on my muscle mass.
Thanks to my newfound energy and confidence, my new fitness goals include more hikes (with increasing difficulty) and a base camp trek.
