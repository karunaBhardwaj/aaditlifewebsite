What I didn’t realize is that I didn’t have the right mix of running and walking, and this was in the way of doing better. In the long run, my routine would have led to a burn-out.

Previously, I’ve participated in marathon training programs that were focused on helping me complete the run. With Aadit Life, it was about fine-tuning and gradually increasing the speeds. While I was now spending less time working out, the results were much more impressive. I lost almost half my body fat and even hit my personal best! It was a milestone I definitely wasn’t expecting to hit.

For me, it was Aadit Life’s focus on long-term wellness that helped the most. Anand didn’t ask me to make any dramatic changes to my diet or fitness routine. He also maintained a steady conversation through the week, which he followed up with regular monitoring of progress, and that made the program truly unique.
